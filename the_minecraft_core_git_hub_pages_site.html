About The Minecraft Core

We’re a community of Minecraft fans of all ages. Whether you’re into survival, creative megabases, command blocks, speedrunning, or Bedwars — you’ll find a place here.

Moderation is active and kind. Keep it family‑friendly. Have fun, help others, and respect everyone.

Rules (Short & Simple)
  1. Be kind. No bullying, hate speech, or harassment.
  2. Keep it family‑friendly. No swearing, slurs, or NSFW.
  3. No spam or self‑promo outside the correct channels.
  4. Follow Discord ToS and community guidelines.
  5. Listen to staff and use common sense.

Breaking rules may result in warnings, mutes, or bans depending on severity.

How to Join

Click the Join the Discord button at the top (or use your invite link). Verify in #welcome, read the rules, and say hi! You can also check the widget for online members.

Tip: If the widget doesn’t load, make sure your browser allows third‑party embeds for discord.com.

FAQ
Is this server kid‑safe?

Yes — moderation is active, swearing/NSFW are not allowed, and we keep a friendly vibe.

Java or Bedrock?

Both! We talk about Java and Bedrock. Events may specify which version they use.

Do you host servers?

We run community SMPs and minigame nights sometimes. Watch #announcements for dates.
